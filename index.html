📋 Key Nat 5 Formulas

📐 Algebra

Quadratic Formula
x = (−b ± √(b²−4ac)) / 2a
Difference of Squares
a²−b² = (a+b)(a−b)
Completing the Square
x²+bx = (x+b/2)² − (b/2)²
Discriminant
b²−4ac > 0: 2 roots | = 0: 1 root | < 0: no roots

📏 Trigonometry

SOH-CAH-TOA
sin=opp/hyp · cos=adj/hyp · tan=opp/adj
Sine Rule
a/sin A = b/sin B = c/sin C
Cosine Rule
a² = b²+c²−2bc·cos A
Area of Triangle
Area = ½ab·sin C
Trig Identity
sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
Standard Angles
sin30°=½ · cos60°=½ · tan45°=1 · sin90°=1

🔵 Geometry

Circle
Area = πr² · Circumference = 2πr = πd
Pythagoras
c² = a² + b²
Volumes
Cylinder: πr²h · Cone: ⅓πr²h · Sphere: ⁴⁄₃πr³
Arc & Sector
Arc = θ/360×2πr · Sector = θ/360×πr²

📊 Statistics

Averages
Mean=Σx/n · Median=middle · Mode=most common
Std Deviation
s = √(Σ(x−x̄)²/(n−1))
Probability
P(A)=fav/total · P(not A)=1−P(A)
IQR
IQR = Q3 − Q1

√ Surds & Indices

Index Laws
aᵐ×aⁿ=aᵐ⁺ⁿ · aᵐ÷aⁿ=aᵐ⁻ⁿ · (aᵐ)ⁿ=aᵐⁿ
Special Indices
a⁰=1 · a⁻ⁿ=1/aⁿ · a^(1/n)=ⁿ√a · a^(m/n)=(ⁿ√a)ᵐ
Surds
√(ab)=√a×√b · Rationalise: ×√b/√b

📈 Functions & Graphs

Straight Line
y = mx + c (m=gradient, c=y-intercept)
Gradient
m = (y₂−y₁)/(x₂−x₁)
Parabola
y=(x−a)²+b: turning point (a,b)

➡ Vectors

Magnitude
|a| = √(x²+y²+z²)
Dot Product
a·b=a₁b₁+a₂b₂+a₃b₃ · a·b=0 → perpendicular
